High profile royal events

Before now, the newer period of the Benin Kingdom had witnessed high profile royal events such as the unprecedented visit of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth 11 to Benin to meet with His Royal Majesty Oba Akenzua in 1956, following that of the Earl of Plymouth who had also earlier visited Oba Akenzua in 1938.
